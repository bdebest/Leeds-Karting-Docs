Leeds Karting Guidelines & Consequences

1. General Community Conduct (Discord & Roblox)

Rule ID Guideline / Policy Severity First Offense Subsequent Offenses
GCC-001 Disrespectful/Toxic Language (Insults, excessive profanity, personal attacks) Medium Verbal Warning / 15-min Chat Mute 1-24h Discord Mute / 1-24h Game Ban
GCC-002 Harassment / Bullying (Targeted, repeated negative behavior, cyberbullying) High 24h Discord Mute & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-003 Hate Speech / Discrimination (Racism, sexism, homophobia, ableism, religious intolerance) Critical Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-004 Spamming / Flooding Chat (Excessive messages, repeated content, reaction spam) Low Verbal Warning / 5-min Chat Mute 1h Discord Mute / 15-min Game Mute
GCC-005 Sharing Personal Information (Doxing, sharing private details of others) Critical Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-006 Impersonation (Staff, other players, official entities) High 24h Discord Mute & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-007 Advertising (Non-Leeds Karting content/links, self-promotion outside designated areas) Medium Warning / Message Deletion 1h Discord Mute / 1h Game Ban
GCC-008 Inappropriate Content (NSFW, gore, disturbing links, shock content) Critical Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-009 Real-World Trading (RWT) / Scamming (Attempting to trade Roblox items/currency for real money/items, or fraudulent activities) Critical Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-010 Threats of Violence / Self-Harm (Any explicit or implicit threats) Critical Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban Permanent Discord Ban & Game Ban
GCC-011 Exploiting Loopholes (Abusing unintended game/system mechanics not covered by direct cheating) High 24h Game Ban / Discord Mute Permanent Game Ban & Discord Ban

2. In-Game Conduct (Roblox Server Specific)

Rule ID Guideline / Policy Severity First Offense Subsequent Offenses
IGC-001 Exploiting / Cheating (Speed hacks, teleporting, direct game manipulation) Critical Permanent Game Ban Permanent Game Ban
IGC-002 Intentional Ramming / Griefing (Deliberately crashing others, blocking paths, destroying property) High 30-min Game Ban / Race Disqualification 24h Game Ban / Permanent Race Ban
IGC-003 Track Cutting / Off-Track Driving (Gaining unfair advantage by leaving track boundaries) Medium Race Disqualification / Warning 1h Game Ban / 24h Race Ban
IGC-004 Ignoring Staff Instructions (During sessions, incidents, or general moderation) Medium Warning / Kick from Server 1h Game Ban
IGC-005 Spawn Camping / Blocking Exits (Preventing players from spawning or leaving areas) Low Verbal Warning / Kick from Server 15-min Game Ban
IGC-006 Abusing In-Game Features (e.g., spamming horns, vehicle glitches for disruption, chat filter bypass) Low Verbal Warning 15-min Game Ban
IGC-007 Inappropriate Roblox Avatar / Username (Offensive, discriminatory, or sexually explicit avatars/names) Medium Warning / Request to Change 24h Game Ban (until changed)
IGC-008 AFK Abuse (Remaining AFK in active sessions to gain rewards or avoid participation) Low Kick from Server 15-min Game Ban
IGC-009 Server Disruption (Actions causing lag, crashes, or severe instability for others) High 24h Game Ban Permanent Game Ban

3. Staff Conduct (Discord & Roblox)

Rule ID Guideline / Policy Severity First Offense Subsequent Offenses
STAFF-001 Breach of Confidentiality (Leaking internal info, unreleased content, private user data) Critical Immediate Termination + Permanent Ban Immediate Termination + Permanent Ban
STAFF-002 Abuse of Power / Tools (Misusing commands, giving unfair advantages, favoritism, unapproved role changes) Critical Immediate Termination + Permanent Ban Immediate Termination + Permanent Ban
STAFF-003 Failure to Follow Chain of Command (Repeatedly bypassing superiors, improper escalation) Medium Verbal Warning / Formal Warning Role Demotion / 24h Suspension
STAFF-004 Inappropriate Staff Conduct (Unprofessional behavior, public arguments, engaging in toxicity with users/staff) Medium Verbal Warning / Formal Warning 24h Suspension / Role Demotion
STAFF-005 Dereliction of Duty (Neglecting assigned tasks, repeated inactivity without notice, failure to meet deadlines) Medium Formal Warning / Meeting with Supervisor 24h Suspension / Role Demotion / Termination Review
STAFF-006 Sharing Staff Credentials (Sharing Discord login, Roblox account, or internal tool access) Critical Immediate Termination + Permanent Ban Immediate Termination + Permanent Ban
STAFF-007 Failure to Report Violations (Witnessed or known violations by players or other staff) High Formal Warning / 24h Suspension Role Demotion / Termination Review
STAFF-008 Conflict of Interest (Using position for personal gain, biased decision-making) High Formal Warning / 24h Suspension Role Demotion / Termination
STAFF-009 Misrepresentation of Authority (Claiming powers/roles not possessed, misleading users) High Formal Warning / 24h Suspension Role Demotion / Termination
STAFF-010 Failure to Document Actions (Not using Reason Box, incomplete logs for moderation/tickets) Medium Formal Warning 24h Suspension / Review of Responsibilities

4. Discord Server Specific Conduct

Rule ID Guideline / Policy Severity First Offense Subsequent Offenses
DSC-001 Discord Terms of Service Violation Critical Permanent Discord Ban Permanent Discord Ban
DSC-002 Unapproved Self-Promotion (Outside designated channels, DMs to members) Low Message Deletion / Verbal Warning 1h Discord Mute
DSC-003 Channel Misuse (Posting off-topic content in specific channels, spamming specific channels) Low Message Deletion / Verbal Warning 15-min Discord Mute
DSC-004 Excessive Pinging / Mentioning (Without valid reason, mass pings) Low Verbal Warning 15-min Discord Mute
DSC-005 Evading Mutes / Bans (Using alternate accounts to bypass moderation) High Permanent Ban on all accounts Permanent Ban on all accounts
DSC-006 Voice Chat Misconduct (Screaming, ear-rape, music spam, disruptive sounds) Medium VC Kick / 15-min VC Mute 1h VC Mute / 24h Discord Mute
DSC-007 Unsolicited DMs (Sending unwanted DMs to members for advertising, harassment, or non-official purposes) Medium Warning / 1h Discord Mute 24h Discord Mute / Permanent Discord Ban
