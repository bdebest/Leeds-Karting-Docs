1. Introduction to Verification

This handbook guides the Leeds Karting Verification Team in ensuring the authenticity and integrity of our community members, particularly in relation to Discord roles, special permissions, and event participation within our Roblox go-karting community. Your role is vital in maintaining a trustworthy and fair environment. The verification process is designed to be streamlined, but your expertise is crucial for handling exceptions and providing support.

2. The Verification Process

The primary verification process at Leeds Karting is automated to provide a quick and efficient experience for our users. However, the Verification Team plays a critical role when the automated system encounters issues or when users require direct assistance.

2.1 Automated Verification Flow

Typically, when a user initiates verification (e.g., by pressing a button on our Discord server), our automated bot sends a unique code directly to the user's Discord Direct Messages (DMs). The user is then prompted to input this code back into the system (e.g., into a text box or a specific command). This process is designed for a single attempt. If the user inputs the code incorrectly, or if the bot encounters an issue, they will need to seek manual assistance by opening a ticket with the Verification Team.

2.2 Manual Verification Team Intervention

The Verification Team steps in when the automated process fails or when a user is confused. Users who cannot complete the automated verification will be instructed to open a ticket with the Verification Team.

When assisting users via tickets, your primary goal is to guide them through the process or manually verify them if necessary.

2.3 Verification Commands & Usage

The Verification Team has access to specific commands to assist users. These commands should be used judiciously and according to policy.

  • `/resetverificationcode [user]`: This command allows you to generate and send a new verification code to a specified user's Discord DMs. Use this if a user reports not receiving the initial code, or if their single attempt failed due to a technical glitch on their end (e.g., accidental incorrect input).
  • `/forceverify [user] [code]`: This command allows you to manually complete the verification for a user by inputting their Discord user ID or username and the correct code.
    • **Important Restriction:** The `/forceverify` command should **ONLY** be used if the user's verification ticket has been open for **over 48 hours**. This policy is in place to encourage users to attempt the automated process independently first and to ensure the Verification Team's time is utilized for genuinely stuck cases, rather than immediate manual overrides. This also ensures users do not feel reliant on staff for a process that is designed to be self-service.
    • Before using `/forceverify`, ensure you have confirmed the user's identity and the validity of the code through secure means (e.g., asking them to confirm details in the ticket that only they would know, or verifying the code they received).

Always document the use of these commands in the ticket's 'Reason Box' or notes section, including the reason for their use.

3. Verification Criteria

The Verification Team must understand the underlying criteria for successful verification. This typically includes:

  • **Valid Discord Account:** The user must possess an active Discord account that is not in violation of Discord's Terms of Service.
  • **Server Membership:** The user must be a member of the official Leeds Karting Discord server.
  • **No History of Severe Violations:** Users with a history of severe rule-breaking within the Leeds Karting community or on Discord may be denied verification for certain roles or privileges.
  • **Age Requirements (if applicable):** Adherence to any age restrictions for certain roles or game features, as determined by Discord or Leeds Karting policy.

Specific criteria for different verification types (e.g., staff role verification vs. general community role verification) will be detailed in relevant departmental guides.

4. Tools & Resources

The Verification Team utilizes several tools and resources to perform their duties effectively. Please note that **we do not use external APIs** for verification processes. All tools are internal to our Discord bot or server management.

  • Ticket System: Our primary platform for receiving and managing user requests for verification assistance.
  • Internal Discord Bot Commands: The `/resetverificationcode` and `/forceverify` commands are essential for direct intervention.
  • Discord User Lookup Tools: Built-in Discord features for verifying Discord usernames, IDs, and checking basic account status within our server.
  • Internal Databases/Logs: Access to logs of past verification attempts or any previous disciplinary actions against a user within our community.
  • Team Communication Channels: Dedicated Discord channels for the Verification Team to discuss complex cases, share knowledge, and seek peer assistance.

5. Confidentiality & Ethics

The Verification Team handles sensitive information. Upholding strict confidentiality and ethical standards is paramount.

  • Data Privacy: Never share any user's Discord ID, username, or any information gathered during the verification process with unauthorized individuals.
  • Impartiality: Conduct all verifications and assistance impartially, without favoritism or bias. Treat all users fairly.
  • Accuracy: Ensure all information recorded and actions taken are accurate and truthful.
  • Security: Protect your own staff account credentials and do not share access to verification tools.
  • Professionalism: Maintain a professional demeanor in all interactions, even when dealing with frustrated users.
